ELIGIBILITY FOR ADMISSION

Under Graduate Courses Applicants who have passed the Higher Secondary Examination or any equivalent examination recognized by the University of Madras are eligible for admission to B.Sc. / B.Com. / B.C.A. / B.A. degree courses.

B.Sc. Computer Science

B.Sc. Computer Science Eligibility. Aspiring students should have completed 10+2 or its equivalent examination with Physics, Chemistry, Physics, and English. Those who have 3 years Diploma Course after S.S.C. or Intermediate Commerce or Arts Examination are also eligible.

B.Sc. Biochemistry

The basic eligibility criterion for pursuing B.Sc. (Biochemistry) degree is qualifying 10+2 or equivalent examination in Science stream with a minimum of 50% marks secured from a recognized board of the country. Also, some reputed colleges and institutes do conduct entrance examination to get admission to their B.Sc.

B.Sc. Visual Communiation

B.Sc. in Visual Communication is a 3-year full-time undergraduate course, the minimum eligibility for admission to which is the successful completion of the Senior Secondary Certificate (10+2) qualification from a recognized educational board, with a minimum aggregate score of 50%.

B.A. Criminology & Police Administration

The eligibility criteria for admission to B.A. Criminology and Police Administration are as follows: All those who have qualified their 10+2 or equivalent examination with a minimum aggregate of 50% marks and above in any relevant stream from a recognized university are eligible for admission.

B.Sc. Electronics & Communication Science

B.Sc. Electronics and Communication is a 3-year full-time undergraduate course. Eligibility for the course is qualified 10+2 examinations with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics subjects or an equivalent examination with 70% of marks from a recognized board.

B.C.A. Bachelor of Computer Applications

Bachelor of computer application [BCA] is equivalent to B. Tech or BE Computer engineering. The students who had passed 10+2 with the minimum marks of 45% to 60% in any recognized board of education are eligible for BCA admission in colleges.

B.Com. Commerce

The basic eligibility required for B.Com. degree in Professional students must have successfully passed 12th Standard/HSC/10+2 (or equivalent Level of Examination) from any recognized Board.

B.A. English

Eligibility For joining a bachelor's degree course, the candidate must have passed the higher secondary school certificate or 10+2 from a recognized board. The duration of the course is usually three years.

B.Lit. Tamil Lit

Minimum eligibility for the course is 10+2 pass from a recognized board or institution. B. Lit is a popular course of study in Tamil, Hindi and English language, however, it can be studied in an Indian or Foreign language.

B.S.W. Social Work

The candidate applying for the course must complete 10+2 or equivalent examination from a recognized board. A few universities like Osmania University, the candidate must have secured at least 40% marks in aggregate. For SC&ST, a pass is enough.

B.B.A. Airline & Airport Management

B.B.A. Airport Management Eligibility Students who have successfully passed 12 Standard/HSC/10+2 (or equivalent Level of Examination) from any recognized Board.

B.Sc. Health Care & Hospital Management

Students who have completed their 10+2 examination or any equivalent qualified examination with above 55% of marks or an equivalent are eligible to apply for Bachelor of Hospital Management course. Students who are eligible can apply for this course.

B.Sc. Automobile Technology

Tech. in Automobile Engineering is an undergraduate engineering degree. Students passed in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as subjects are eligible to take admission in this course. The duration of the course is four years.

B.Sc. Physics

B.Sc. Physics is an undergraduate program dealing with the nuances of Physics and its various properties. The course duration is 3 years. The minimum eligibility for pursuing this course is an intermediate 10+2 in Science with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology and English as compulsory subjects.

B.B.A. Business Administration

The basic eligibility criteria for admission to the BBA course is an aggregate of 50% marks in 10+2 from a recognized educational board. In many colleges, admission to this course is done on the basis of the entrance exam but some colleges do offer admission on merit basis.

B.Sc. Hotel Management & Catering Tech.

Applicants who have taken science with or without mathematics in higher secondary examination are eligible

B.Sc. Mathematics

B.Sc. Mathematics: Eligibility. The minimum eligibility criterion needed to be met for pursuing this course is a 10+2 or equivalent qualification in Science or Commerce stream. The education should have been done from a recognized educational board with a minimum score of 50% in Mathematics.

MASTER DEGREE COURSES

M.Sc Biochemistry

Eligibility Of the Master of Science [M.Sc] (Biochemistry) In order to become eligible for M.Sc. Biochemistry, the candidate should have Bachelor's degree with minimum 50% marks aggregate in Chemistry, Biochemistry, Botany, Zoology, Microbiology and other Life Sciences subjects.

M.Sc Computer Science

M.Sc. Computer Applications Eligibility. Aspiring students should have passed a Bachelor's Degree of minimum three years duration from a recognized university in any discipline with minimum 50% marks. Mathematics at 10+2 School level or Graduation level is compulsory

M.A. English

M.A. English is a 2-year post graduate course in English language and literature. ... The minimum eligibility criterion required for the candidates is to possess a Bachelor's degree in English or relevant discipline from a recognized university with a minimum aggregate score of 60%.

M.Sc. Mathematics

The eligibility criteria for M.Sc. Mathematics course is graduation in any discipline with Mathematics and English as the main subjects with a minimum 50% aggregate. However, institutes prefer students with a B.Sc. /B.E. /B. Tech

M.Com. Commerce

For applying M.Com includes graduation in Commerce with a minimum of 50% marks. Students who have passed B.B.S., B.B.A., B.F.I.A., and B.B.E. with at least 60% marks can also apply. Certain universities also admit students with a B.A.

M.S.W. Social Work

A candidate who has passed B.S.W. / B.A. degree examination fromunder a recognised university can see MSW admission. A candidate who has secured minimum of 50% aggregate marks in B.S.W. / B.A. degree under a recognised university has the required MSW course eligibility.

PROFESSIONAL COURSES

M.Sc. Health Care & Hospital Management

Eligibility Candidates should have a Bachelor's degree in MBBS/BDS/BAMS/BHMS/BNYS/BPT/BSc Nursing (Basic or Post Basic)/BPharm/BSc in Allied Health Sciences/Bachelor degree in Arts/ Commerce/Science/Law from a recognised university with a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate.

M.B.A. Finance - Marketing - System - HR - International

Candidates aspiring to pursue the course need to have completed graduation in any stream. In most cases, qualification of a relevant entrance test GMAT, CAT, XAT, MAT, etc. The minimum eligible score may vary across institutes.

RESEARCH PROGRAMMES

M.Phil. English

M. Phil. in English is a 2-year postgraduate program essentially involving a supervised research-based project in the English literature. Students enrolled to the course have the option of choosing from among a variety of areas of research within the discipline.

M.Phil. Commerce

M. Phil. (Commerce) is a two-year post-graduation course. ... Following is the list of some institutes that offer this course: Alagappa Government Arts College, Tamil Nadu.

M.Phil. Computer Science

M. Phil. Computer Science is a 1 year post graduate program which is aimed at developing skilled professionals and contribute to the IT sector with excellence and ingenuity. The basic eligibility of the course a post graduate degree in Computer Science discipline from a recognized university.

Ph.D. English

Ph. D. in English is a 2-year doctorate degree in English, essentially consisting of research work. Upon completion of the course, for procurement of the degree, scholars are needed to submit a detailed thesis.

Ph.D. Commerce

PhD in Commerce and Management is a 3-year doctorate level course in Commerce and Business Management. To qualify for admission to the program, the eligibility criteria is to have completed the post-graduation degree in commerce or should hold an M. Phil.

Ph.D. Computer Science

Doctor of Philosophy (Ph. ... Ph. D. in Computer Science is a 3-year long doctorate level degree certification in the area of computer science and its related aspects and the minimum eligibility criterion for admission is 55% marks in M. Phil or any other equivalent level subject.
